It looks like you might be eligible for the Core Strength - Local Communities initiative.

This initiative aims to support small locally led groups having a sustained beneficial impact on the lives of people who are excluded or disadvantaged. Funding invests in the organisation as a whole, including covering basic running costs. This initiative doesn’t fund direct project delivery. Grants are available between £1000 and £10,000.
