Core Strength - Local Communities

This initiative aims to support small locally led groups having a sustained beneficial impact on the lives of people who are excluded or disadvantaged. Funding will invest in the organisation as a whole, including covering basic running costs. This initiative doesn’t fund direct project delivery. Your organisation must be based in the UK and your income must have been below £100,000 in your last financial year. Grants are available between £1,000 and £10,000.
