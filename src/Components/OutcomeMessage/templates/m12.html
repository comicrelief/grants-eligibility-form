You might be eligible for 2 initiatives!

 

Safe, Secure, Settled

We will support projects using sport to improve the lives of young people without a safe or secure place to live. Grants are available between £100,000 and £500,000. Your project must be working toward at least one of the following outcomes:

  • Children and young people increase and/or sustain their engagement with education
  • Children and young people have increased pathways into training or employment
  • Children and young people increase life skills
  • More children and young people have a safe and supportive network or trusted place to go for advice

Core Strength - Local Communities

This initiative aims to support small locally led groups having a sustained beneficial impact on the lives of people who are excluded or disadvantaged. Funding invests in the organisation as a whole, including covering basic running costs. This initiative doesn’t fund direct project delivery. Your organisation’s income must have been below £100,000 in your last financial year. Grants are available between £1,000 and £10,000.
